Frequently Asked Questions

What is KEPHSA Research Hub?

KEPHSA Research Hub is a platform dedicated to fostering research, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing among pharmacy students and professionals.

Who can participate in KEPHSA Research Hub?

Any pharmacy student, researcher, or professional interested in contributing to or learning from the research community can join.

How do I join?

Click the Be a member toggle on our website

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, membership is ksh 200 with annual subscription.
