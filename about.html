Welcome to KEPhSA Research Hub

The KEPhSA Research Hub is dedicated to empowering and inspiring pharmacy students to excel in research and healthcare advancements. Founded in 2023, the hub provides a dynamic platform for collaboration, innovation, and mentorship among pharmacy students across Kenya.

Patron

Prof. Ermias Terefe

Program Leader and Assistant Professor of Pharmacology and Therapeutics at USIU-Africa.

Dr. Terefe is a distinguished consultant pharmacist, curriculum expert, and instructional designer with extensive contributions to pharmacy education and healthcare development.
