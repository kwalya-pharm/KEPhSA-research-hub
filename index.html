Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide pharmacy students with a dynamic platform to explore, experiment, and excel in research.

Vision: To empower and inspire the next generation of pharmacy leaders in Kenya through impactful research contributions.

What We Offer

  • Access to research facilities and equipment
  • Mentorship from experienced researchers
  • Networking with pharmacy professionals
  • Publication opportunities

Membership Benefits

  • Exclusive access to events and workshops
  • Regular research opportunity updates
  • Dedicated project support
  • Publication assistance
  • Specialized training sessions

How to Join

Membership requires a one-time fee of Ksh 200 plus annual subscription. Open to all Kenyan pharmacy students.
