The KEPhSA Research Hub

An affiliate of the Kenya Pharmaceutical Student's Organization (KEPhSA)

Vision

Empower and inspire the next generation of pharmacy students by establishing a dynamic research hub.

Mission

Provide pharmacy students with a platform to explore, experiment, and excel, ultimately shaping the future of healthcare through their research contributions.

Objectives

  • Access to research facilities and equipment
  • Mentorship from experienced researchers
  • Networking among pharmacy students
  • Advancement of pharmacy profession in Kenya

Membership

Requirements

  • Non-refundable registration fee of Ksh 200
  • Annual subscription to the Hub

Rights & Responsibilities

Members may attend meetings, receive updates, access support, publish articles, and undergo specialized training.

Termination

Membership may end by resignation, deregistration, or disciplinary action in accordance with the Constitution.

Disciplinary Action

Complaints must be submitted in writing for review by the Executive Committee.

Leadership

All members are eligible to be nominated for the Executive Committee.
