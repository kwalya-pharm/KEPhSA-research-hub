Welcome to KEPhSA Research Hub

Connecting Pharmacy Students with Research Opportunities

An affiliate of the Kenya Pharmaceutical Student's Organization (KEPhSA)

VISION

Empower and inspire the next generation of pharmacy students by establishing a dynamic research hub.

MISSION

Provide pharmacy students with a platform to explore, experiment, and excel, ultimately shaping the future of healthcare through their research contributions.

OBJECTIVES

  • Provide pharmacy students with access to research facilities and equipment.
  • Offer mentorship and guidance from experienced researchers.
  • Promote collaboration and networking among pharmacy students and researchers.
  • Contribute to the advancement of the pharmacy profession in Kenya.

MEMBERSHIP

Requirements for Membership

All pharmacy students from all local chapters are eligible to be members.

To become a full member, one:

  • is required to pay a non-refundable registration fee of Ksh 200
  • should subscribe to the KEPhSARH annually

Membersâ€™ Rights and Responsibilities

Every member shall have a right to:

  1. Attend any meeting of the Hub
  2. Receive any information which it is competent for the executive team to give
  3. Receive at the discretion of the Executive Committee such assistance in any matter as may be rendered in terms of this Constitution
  4. Take part in any discussion on any matter at any general or special meeting
  5. Write research-related articles for public/professional education
  6. Attend specialized research trainings aimed at impacting research skills
  7. Full Members shall be entitled to exercise the following additional rights, limited to Full members only:
    1. To vote on any motion put to a General or Special Meeting of the Hub
    2. To nominate members of the Executive Committee or of Special Committees
    3. If qualified, to be nominated for and appointed to any office or committee of the Hub

Termination of Membership

Every member shall remain a member until their membership is terminated in accordance with the provisions herein contained:

  1. The membership of any person shall automatically be terminated:
    1. Upon the deregistration from the School of Pharmacy in which they study, which is the basis of their admission to membership
    2. Upon receipt of a letter from the Executive Committee accepting their written resignation as a member
    3. Upon removal of their name by the Council from the Roll of Members in accordance with its powers under this Constitution
  2. No subscription or levy or part thereof shall be refunded to any member upon the termination of their membership.
  3. A member may resign by giving notice in writing to the Hub. The Executive Committee shall be entitled to waive the whole or any part of the period of notice and/or subscription fee due for such period.
  4. A member shall only be subject to expulsion if such expulsion occurs either pursuant to any disciplinary proceedings that may have been instituted against a member.
  5. Any entity who was formerly a member of the Hub and whose membership has terminated for any reason may be re-admitted at the discretion of the Executive Committee and Council, who shall be entitled to rule on the re-admission of any member and to impose, in their sole discretion, terms, conditions, or restrictions to such re-admission they may determine.
  6. The Executive Committee shall also be entitled, in its sole and absolute discretion, to reinstate any member who was previously expelled by the Hub in accordance with the relevant regulations.

Discipline and Disciplinary Action

All complaints are to be channeled to the Executive Committee by writing. The Executive shall deliberate the matter in accordance with the Constitution.

LEADERSHIP

All members are eligible to be nominated as part of the Executive Committee.
