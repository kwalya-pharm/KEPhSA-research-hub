Our Purpose

Our Mission

To provide pharmacy students with a dynamic platform to explore, experiment, and excel, shaping the future of healthcare through impactful research contributions.

Our Vision

To empower and inspire the next generation of pharmacy leaders in Kenya by establishing a vibrant, accessible research hub for all students.

Our Core Objectives

  • Provide access to essential research facilities and equipment.
  • Connect students with experienced researchers for mentorship.
  • Facilitate networking among pharmacy students and professionals.
  • Contribute to the advancement of the pharmacy profession in Kenya.

Become a Member

Join our growing community and unlock a world of opportunities. Membership is open to all pharmacy students in Kenya.

Membership Benefits

  • Exclusive access to meetings and events.
  • Regular updates on research opportunities.
  • Dedicated support for your research projects.
  • Opportunities to publish your articles and findings.
  • Specialized training and workshops.

How to Join

Membership requires a one-time registration fee of Ksh 200 and an annual subscription to the Hub. Detailed information on rights, responsibilities, and our constitution is available upon joining.There will be a call for membership. Stay tuned!
