Homepage

The KEPhSA Research Hub is dedicated to empowering and inspiring pharmacy students to excel in research and healthcare advancements. Founded in 2023, the hub provides a dynamic platform for collaboration, innovation, and mentorship among pharmacy students across Kenya.

Our Vision

Empower and inspire the next generation of pharmacy professionals by establishing a dynamic research hub.

Our Mission

Provide pharmacy students with a platform to explore, experiment, and excel, ultimately shaping the future of healthcare through their research contributions.

Research Bootcamp Series

Introduction to Research with Dr. Gareth Dyke

We had an insightful introduction to research session with Dr. Gareth Dyke. The session covered foundational aspects of conducting impactful research.

Literature Review with Dr. Norhayati M. Farm

Our second session, hosted by Dr. Norhayati M. Farm, explored the critical process of literature reviews in research, emphasizing best practices and methodologies. The webinar covered topics including effective search strategies, PubMed, MeSH, and Mendeley software for citation management.

Cervical Cancer Awareness

Cervical cancer is one of the most preventable types of cancer. Regular screenings, HPV vaccinations, and public awareness campaigns can significantly reduce its impact. Join us in spreading awareness and saving lives.
