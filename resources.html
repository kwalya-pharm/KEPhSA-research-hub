Recommended Platforms and Software

Below is a curated list of digital tools essential for pharmacy students conducting research:

  • Zotero – Free reference management software for organizing citations and bibliographies. Visit Zotero
  • Mendeley – Reference manager with social networking capabilities for researchers. Visit Mendeley
  • GraphPad Prism – Widely used for biostatistics and graphing in biomedical sciences. Visit GraphPad
  • SPSS – IBM’s statistical package for the social sciences, valuable for data analysis. Visit SPSS
  • Overleaf – An online LaTeX editor ideal for writing scientific papers. Visit Overleaf
  • Google Scholar – A search engine for scholarly literature, indispensable for sourcing academic references. Visit Google Scholar
